What is Sprunki Retake?

Sprunki Retake is a horror-themed music creation mod for Incredibox that transforms the cheerful rhythm game into a dark and suspenseful experience. Players can create unique musical compositions while exploring hidden horror elements.

Through drag-and-drop mechanics, players can trigger the "Parasite Mode" using special items, experiencing spine-chilling visual and audio effects that completely transform the game's atmosphere.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sprunki Retake?

Sprunki Retake is a horror-themed mod for Incredibox that transforms the original rhythm game into a dark musical experience with unique horror elements and parasite mechanics.

How do I trigger Parasite Mode?

To enter Parasite Mode, find and use special items like the black hat or red branch on specific characters. This will transform both the visual and audio aspects of the game.

Is Sprunki Retake free to play?

Yes, the base mod is completely free to play. Some additional sound packs and features may be available as premium content.

Can I share my creations?

Yes, you can share your musical compositions with the community and collaborate with other players through various sharing features.

What platforms are supported?

Sprunki Retake is available on both mobile devices (Android/iOS) and web browsers, with optimized controls for each platform.
